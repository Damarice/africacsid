## Color Palette

### Primary Colors:
- **Primary Green:** #2D5F3F (buttons, accents)
- **Secondary Blue:** #1E5A8E (links, secondary elements)
- **Accent Orange:** #E67E22 (highlights, CTAs)

### Neutral Colors:
- **Dark Gray:** #2C3E50 (footer, text)
- **Medium Gray:** #7F8C8D (secondary text)
- **Light Gray:** #F8F9FA (section backgrounds)
- **White:** #FFFFFF (cards, main background)

### Semantic Colors:
- **Success:** #27AE60 (confirmations)
- **Warning:** #F39C12 (alerts)
- **Error:** #E74C3C (errors)

---

## Typography

### Headings:
- **Font:** Inter, Poppins, or Montserrat (sans-serif)
- **H1:** 48px (desktop), 32px (mobile)
- **H2:** 36px (desktop), 28px (mobile)
- **H3:** 24px (desktop), 20px (mobile)

### Body:
- **Font:** Inter or Open Sans
- **Size:** 16px (minimum)
- **Line Height:** 1.6

### Buttons:
- **Font:** Bold, 16px
- **Padding:** 12px 32px
- **Border Radius:** 4px

---

## Responsive Breakpoints

- **Mobile:** < 640px (1 column)
- **Tablet:** 640px - 1024px (2 columns)
- **Desktop:** > 1024px (3+ columns)

---

## Animations & Interactions

### On Scroll:
- Counter numbers animate up
- Sections fade in from bottom
